How Do Your Clothes Impact the Environment?

Some love fashion, some do not. But, everyone needs clothes! Our goal is to show you how your shopping habits, before and after, contribute to climate change. What can you do to minimize these effects?

What is fast fashion?

Fast fashion is inexpensive clothing produced rapidly in response to the latest trends. Most clothing brands do not give much thought or action to creating clothing that does not harm the environment

What is wrong with fast fashion?

Retailers often use cheap, toxic dye and massive amounts of water (in addition to other negative practices) in order to meet demands. These factors contribute to climate change.
